# Code of Conduct
We expect work in this organization to stay respectful, direct, and technically grounded.

## Expected behavior
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements in terms of evidence, risks, and tradeoffs.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and scoped to the code, design, or process under review.
- Use inclusive language and avoid personal attacks, harassment, or pile-ons.
- Surface safety, security, privacy, and reliability concerns early instead of working around them silently.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Repeated bad-faith argument, trolling, or derailing technical review.
- Sharing credentials, sensitive data, or private material in public channels without permission.

## Scope
This applies to pull requests, issues, discussions, review comments, demos, and other organization spaces.

## Reporting
If you need help with a conduct issue, contact the repository owners or organization owners directly.
